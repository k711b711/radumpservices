About Us

RA Dump Services LLC is a leading dump trailer solutions provider located in Ravenel, close to Charleston, South Carolina. We specialize in offering top-notch hauling services for various needs, including construction, landscaping, and more.

Our company was born out of Roofing America Sheet Metal Construction, a reputable roofing services provider. Leveraging our experience and expertise in the construction industry, we expanded into the hauling business to meet the growing needs of our clients.
