Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions

What are your business hours?

Our business hours are Monday to Friday, 8:00 AM to 5:00 PM.

Do you offer delivery services?

Yes, we offer delivery services within a certain radius. Additional charges may apply.

Can I rent a dump trailer for personal use?

Yes, our dump trailers are available for both commercial and personal use.

Do you provide assistance with loading and unloading?

We do not provide assistance with loading and unloading. It is the renter's responsibility to safely load and unload the trailer.

Rental Questions

What is the rental period?

The rental period is typically 24 hours, but longer rental periods are available upon request.

Is insurance required for rentals?

Yes, we require renters to provide proof of insurance covering the rented equipment.

Can I extend my rental period?

Yes, you can extend your rental period. Please contact us to make arrangements.

Payment Questions

What forms of payment do you accept?

We accept cash, check, and credit card payments.

Do you require a deposit?

Yes, we require a deposit for rentals. The amount varies depending on the equipment rented.

Is the deposit refundable?

Yes, the deposit is refundable upon return of the rented equipment in good condition.
