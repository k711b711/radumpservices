Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions

What are your business hours?

Our business hours are Monday to Friday, 8:00 AM to 5:00 PM.

Do you offer delivery services?

Yes, we offer delivery services within a certain radius. Additional charges may apply.

Can I rent a dump trailer for personal use?

Yes, our dump trailers are available for both commercial and personal use.

Do you provide assistance with unloading?

We provide assistance with unloading. Please call us when your dump is full to schedule a pickup.

Rental Questions

What is included in the rental?

The rental includes 9 days and two tons. Anything over carries a fee.

Is insurance required for rentals?

No, insurance is not required for rentals, but it is recommended.

Payment Questions

How do I pay for my rental?

We accept cash, check, and credit card payments. Payment is due at the time of pickup or delivery.

Are there any additional fees?

Additional fees may apply for exceeding the rental period or weight limit. Please contact us for more details.

Can I cancel my rental reservation?

Yes, you can cancel your reservation.
